Top three activities to do at Hyderabad

Visit the Old City to experience the historical heritage

The Old City have the historical places like Charminar, Golcanda fort, Falaknuma Palace, Salarjung Museum.

Go out for a picinc to Tank Bund during night times

Tank Bund is the perfect place for the picnic with the iconic constructions like Hussain Sagar, Secretariat, NTR Gardens.

Experience the Next Generation Hyderabad

Gachibowli, Kokapet, Madhapur have the world class infrastructure making the place to feel like a Foreign city.
